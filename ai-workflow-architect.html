Workflow Summary

  • Frame a falsifiable hypothesis before touching any AI tool — the workflow exists to test claims, not confirm hopes.
  • Layer evidence: desk research → real conversations → a smoke test → a lightweight prototype. Each stage should raise or lower your confidence measurably.
  • Treat every AI output (market size, persona, survey draft) as a first draft requiring your judgment, not a verdict.
  • Decision point: you need at least one channel with real signal (sign-ups, paid pre-orders, or repeat interview demand) before writing production code.
  • Total realistic timeline for a solo founder working part-time: 2–4 weeks end-to-end.
